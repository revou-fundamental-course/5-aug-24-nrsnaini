Why Use BMI Calculator?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a straightforward measure that estimates body fat based on your weight and height. It is calculated by dividing weight in kilograms by their height in metres squared = kg/m2

Using BMI calculator offers several benefits:

  • Track your weight
  • Support your diet plan
  • Health awareness

The BMI calculator is designed for adults. Pregnant or breastfeeding women should not rely on BMI results. Always consult with a healthcare professional for personalized advice.
